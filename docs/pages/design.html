Design

The dataset that we chose is a UK Consumer Dataset from the years 1997 - 2022. This is a vast dataset so we had narrow down what areas of the data we sought to analyse. We decided to only anaylse the data between the years 1997 and 2017 as we felt as if 20 years was a sufficient sample period of time. Another reason fro omitting the years from 2018 - 2022 was the COVID-19 pandemic that shock the global economy, subsequently affecting the consumer spending.

Hypothesis

We aim to investigate how time of year affects consumer spending across different categories.
